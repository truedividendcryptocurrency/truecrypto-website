Cryptocurrency offers the possibility of transactional freedom to everyone on the planet.

For the first time in history it's possible for individuals to transact with other individuals in the world without involving any 3rd parties in a completely trustless manner. Everyone should have the opportunity to be able to take part in this financial revolution, and cryptocurrency networks will facilitate this by enabling instant and immutable transactions to anyone in the world.

Open source enables unrestricted access.

Anyone is able to download and use the software, free of charge, forever while leveraging the power of peer-to-peer networking and a dedicated community of open source developers which reduce coding errors and increase the security of the software.

Masternodes improve the network.

Active network participants with the appropriate minimum balance can improve the network connectivity by operating a masternode. Masternode operators receive continuous rewards in exchange for being active on the network.

Anyone can verify their own transactions.

A block explorer enables quick and easy access to the data stored in the blockchain which allows anyone to verify their own transactions. Our block explorers are graciously provided by chainmapper.com and openchains.info. We also have a backup explorer running at explorer.truedividendcrypto.org.

Project Team

Development Lead

Alan Rudolf (aka @suprnurd) is a developer from Montreal, Quebec in Canada. With a background in logistics and process management, his extensive expertise in both hardware and software computer systems make him an ideal candidate to produce the IT solutions required to solve the problems that arise when working on a cryptocurrency project. Alan has extensive experience with system administration and development operations, and also happens to have skills in video game development - both desktop and mobile. He also has an extensive multimedia background, and enjoys working with graphics and sound as much as with code.

He has been involved in the cryptosphere since the summer of 2017, when he was first introduced to Bitcoin. He immediately fell in love with its simplicity and ease of use, and quickly was swept up in the crypto craze that was 2017. He experimented with GPU mining and got involved with the Chaincoin project. He spent a long time assisting with the community support for Chaincoin, and became highly proficient in masternode troubleshooting as a result. Throughout the past several years he has been involved with various cryptocurrency projects in various capacities, and most recently has decided to contribute to the improvement of True Dividend Crypto.
